Korean Kimchi

Explore Korean Kimchi

Discover the different types of Korean kimchi and their unique flavors.

Baechu Kimchi

The most popular Korean cabbage kimchi, fermented with rich red pepper flakes and garlic.

Kkakdugi

Crunchy diced radish kimchi, packed with refreshing sweetness and deep flavors.

Oi Sobagi

Fresh cucumber kimchi filled with aromatic chives, perfect for crisp summer days.

Baek Kimchi

Non-spicy white kimchi submerged in cold, savory brine, loved by beginners.

Chonggak Kimchi

Traditional young radish kimchi with crisp greens, offering an incredible crunch.

Yeolmu Kimchi

Refreshing, light, and tangy young radish leaf kimchi, amazing with cold summer noodles.
